BoilerVerse Patch Notes v2.5.3

Status:   Mostly stable
Released: Just after midterms (roughly)
Build ID: @B01L3R_25F

Message for Players

BoilerVerse is still in open beta, which is a polite way of saying the dev team is still duct-taping things together. Expect missing features, unbalanced gameplay, and occasional reality glitches.

Thanks for playtesting, your continued interaction is valuable feedback.

New Features

  • Added "Cold Resistance" passive skill for students walking from WALC to PMU in January.
  • Introduced Dynamic Side Quests:
    • Find an empty outlet in the Union.
    • Remember why you walked into WALC 3rd floor.
  • Implemented Squirrel AI 2.0, now with advanced pathfinding and sandwich theft behavior.
  • Laundry Simulation now supports multiple cycles of existential dread.
  • Added realistic sleep deprivation shaders (applied automatically during finals).
  • Coffee machines now produce "Extra Life" buffs on critical hits.

Balancing

  • Increased Homework Drop Rate by 12%.
  • Nerfed "Free Time", now triggers only between 2-4 AM.
  • "Participation Points" now scale logarithmically with eye contact.
  • Adjusted Caffeine Tolerance curve; exponential decay now more accurate.
  • Buffed Group Projects to include optional "solo play" mode (buggy).
  • Rebalanced dining hall food stats:
    • "Mystery Meat" now provides +2 Constitution but -5 Trust.
    • "Soft Serve Machine" remains unpatched, may crash without warning.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue where students would enter the wrong classroom and stay out of pride.
  • Resolved glitch where professors forget Brightspace exists until Week 7.
  • Patched bug causing scooter NPCs to ignore physical laws near the Union.
  • Fixed NPC duplication near the Co-Rec treadmills.
  • Addressed infinite loop when asking "How's your semester going?"
  • Removed deprecated feature: "Free Printing Quotas."
  • Adjusted Wi-Fi hitbox to extend 3 more inches beyond doorways.

Known Issues

  • Campus squirrels remain sentient; negotiation ongoing.
  • Motivation meter does not regenerate naturally.
  • Certain students still attempt to use elevators for one floor.
  • Printing demons persist in basement labs.
  • Reports of spectral figure haunting WALC at 3 AM (marked Won't Fix).
  • Still can't find memory for more parking.

Developer Notes

We've heard your feedback, ignored some of it, and implemented the rest with duct tape and optimism.

Stay hydrated. Save often. And remember: the semester is still in early access.
